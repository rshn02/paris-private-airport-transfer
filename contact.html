Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions

How to book a transfer?

You can book directly online via our booking form, by phone or WhatsApp. For urgent bookings (less than 2 hours), prefer phone.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments by cash or credit card directly to the driver.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled transfer time. Modifications are free up to 12 hours before departure.

Do prices include all fees?

Yes, our prices are all-inclusive: driver, fuel, highway tolls, parking, and insurance. No hidden fees.

Do you provide invoices for businesses?

Absolutely! We issue detailed invoices for all our services. For businesses, we can establish agreements with monthly billing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What happens in case of flight delay?

We track your flight in real-time and automatically adjust the pickup time. We offer 1 hour of free waiting after the actual landing.
