Terms and Conditions

General terms applicable to all Paris Private Airport Transfer services.

Article 1 – Purpose

These Terms and Conditions govern all chauffeur-driven transport services provided by Paris Private Airport Transfer (the “Provider”) to any customer (the “Customer”).

Article 2 – Booking

Bookings may be made via the online form, by phone, or by email. A booking is confirmed upon receipt of a confirmation message from the Provider. For bookings made less than 2 hours before the scheduled service, confirmation must be provided by phone.

Article 3 – Rates

All rates are quoted in euros including VAT. Rates include chauffeur service, fuel, tolls, parking, and insurance. Additional options (child seat, meet-and-greet, etc.) are charged separately and clearly indicated at booking.

For long-distance journeys, a personalized quote is prepared on request. Quotes are valid for 30 days.

Article 4 – Payment Methods

The Provider accepts the following payment methods:

  • Cash (paid to the driver at the end of the service)
  • Bank card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Bank transfer (corporate invoices, 30-day terms)
  • Company cheque

Article 5 – Cancellation and Changes

Cancellation: Canceling more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time is free. For late cancellations (less than 24 hours) or no-shows, 50% of the agreed fare will be charged.

Changes: Changes to the date, time or pickup location are free up to 12 hours before the service, subject to availability.

Article 6 – Provider Obligations

  • Provide a clean, high-end vehicle in excellent condition
  • Present a professional chauffeur holding a valid VTC license
  • Respect the agreed pickup time and location
  • Maintain strict confidentiality regarding the Customer’s journey and information
  • Hold the mandatory insurance coverage required for VTC services

Article 7 – Customer Obligations

  • Be present or reachable at the agreed pickup time and location
  • Provide accurate information when booking
  • Not transport hazardous materials in the vehicle
  • Refrain from smoking in the vehicle
  • Respect the driver and the vehicle equipment

Article 8 – Airport Waiting Time

One hour of free waiting is included after the actual landing time (real-time flight tracking). Beyond this, a supplement of €30 per hour or part thereof will apply.

Article 9 – Liability

The Provider holds professional liability insurance. The Provider is not liable in cases of force majeure, exceptional weather conditions, or events outside its control (third-party accidents, roadblocks, etc.).

Article 10 – Personal Data

Personal data collected during booking is used only to provide the service. It is never sold to third parties. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Article 11 – Complaints

Any complaint must be sent by email to contact@rmprestige.fr within 48 hours of the service.

Article 12 – Applicable Law and Disputes

These terms are governed by French law. In case of dispute, an amicable solution will be sought. If necessary, the competent courts of Beauvais (Oise) will have jurisdiction.
