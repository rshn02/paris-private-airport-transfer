Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Data Controller

Paris Private Airport Transfer
Contact: parisprivateairporttransfer@gmail.com

2. Data Collected

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Identity: first and last name
  • Contact details: email, phone number, pickup address
  • Booking details: date, time, route, vehicle type, options
  • Flight information: flight number, terminal (if applicable)

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used to:

  • Process and confirm your bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and trip updates
  • Issue invoices
  • Improve the quality of our services
  • Contact you in case of urgent changes or information

4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on the performance of the transport contract you concluded with us (GDPR article 6(1)(b)). For marketing communications, processing is based on your consent.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for 3 years after the last service, in accordance with accounting and tax obligations. Invoicing data is kept for 10 years in compliance with commercial law.

6. Data Recipients

Your data is never sold to third parties. It may be shared with our technical subcontractors (hosting, email) only to provide the service, in compliance with GDPR.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data (under conditions)
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: object to certain processing
  • Restriction: request limitation of processing

To exercise these rights, contact us by email at parisprivateairporttransfer@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL: www.cnil.fr

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. Our site uses HTTPS to secure all data exchanges.

9. Cookies

This website does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Only strictly necessary technical cookies may be used for the site to function properly.

10. Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. The version available on this site is the current one.
